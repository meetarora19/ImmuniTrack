About ImmuniTrack

ImmuniTrack is a comprehensive digital health and immunization tracking system designed to ensure nationwide healthcare access and improved vaccination rates. With features such as digitized vaccination records, QR code-based certification, and blockchain-secured data storage, ImmuniTrack empowers families to manage and verify their health records easily and securely.

It tackles critical issues like missed vaccinations, vaccine shortages, and fake certificates by offering real-time reminders, predictive analytics for supply chain optimization, and multilingual geolocation alerts for nearby vaccination camps.
